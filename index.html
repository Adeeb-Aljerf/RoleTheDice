Welcome to Roll the Dice!

Game Rules:

  • Current score accumulates with each roll if you hold the score
  • If a player rolls a 1, they lose their current score and it's the next player's turn
  • Players can choose to 'Hold' to save the progress but then it's the next player's turn
  • First player to reach 50 points wins!
